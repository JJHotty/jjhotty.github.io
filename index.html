Who I am...

Kia Ora, My name is Jas and this year I am studying at Dev Academy Aoteroa.
I live in the South Island of New Zealand and get to gaze at the most beautiful stary nights, get to enjoy the crisp fresh air, and love the outdoors.

Why am I studying this year? - I decided to study this year due to a realisation that life will never be suitable for a career change. Things like that aren't given, but taken through a ton of effort and hard work.

Why software Development? - I have always been a bit of a nerd. Since I was about 12, I've dabbled in software development playing around and having fun. But I never really pursued it as a viable career option. I have always kept up to date though with all the latest technologies, I've used linux as my primary desktop for more than 10 years and have worked as a technician for a local electronics store too. I have a big passion for these related fields and I'm excited to embark on the next chapter.

What are your goals? - My goal for this year is to work overtime. To really put in all the time and effort that I can afford and score my first development job before the end of the course. I also have an interest in learning a few development stacks. I have had my sights on the MERN stack first, but I'd then like to branch out and pick up whatever I can.

What is this blog about? - This blog is an opportunity for me to put on paper the big list of things that I'm doing, learning and all the times I've failed. And I can't wait to post them here and for others to get to laugh along while I explore and enjoy the journey.

Thanks for checking in! And feel free to explore..
