Who I am...

Kia Ora, My name is Jas and this year I am studying at Dev Academy Aoteroa.
I live in the South Island of New Zealand and get to gaze at the most beautiful stary nights, get to enjoy the crisp fresh air, and love the outdoors.

Why am I studying this year? - I decided to study this year due to a realisation that life will never be suitable for a career change. Things like that aren't given, but taken through a ton of effort and hard work.

Why software Development? - I have always been a bit of a geek, but more than that I have always dabbled in software design (ever since I was about 12). I have used linux as my primary desktop for more than 10 years and it's definitely something I follow closely and am really passionate about.

What are your goals? - My goal for this year is to work overtime. To really put in all the time and effort that I can afford and score my first development job before the end of it. I'd also like to learn a few full development stacks. I have had my sights on the MERN stack. And a couple others.

What is this blog about? - This blog is an opportunity for me to put on paper a large list of things that I am doing, learning and all the times I've failed. And I can't wait to post them here and for others to get to laugh along while I explore and enjoy journey.

Thanks for checking in! And feel free to explore..
